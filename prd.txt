# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

**FreeThings (freethings.com)**

## One‑Liner

A trusted hub that lists **legitimate free rewards, items, and no‑investment earning opportunities** from games and the internet — updated and verified.

---

## 1. Problem Statement

People constantly search for:

* Legit **free in‑game rewards** (chests, emotes, bonuses)
* **No‑investment ways to earn online**

But the internet is full of:

* Scammy websites
* Fake generators
* Outdated or misleading information

Users waste time, risk bans, or lose trust.

---

## 2. Product Goal

Create a **high‑trust, SEO‑focused directory website** that:

* Lists **only legitimate free items and rewards**
* Explains *how* to get them clearly
* Updates content regularly
* Monetizes via ads and later affiliate partnerships

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary

* Gamers (mobile & PC)
* Casual users searching for “free rewards”
* Young users (16–30)

### Secondary

* People looking for side income
* Beginners in online earning
* Users in emerging markets

---

## 4. Core Content Hubs

### 4.1 Games Hub

Path: `/games`

Purpose:

* Central place for **official, legit in‑game freebies**

Examples:

* Clash Royale
* Brawl Stars
* Fortnite
* PUBG Mobile

Sub‑routes:

```
/games/clash-royale
/games/brawl-stars
```

---

### 4.2 Money Hub

Path: `/money`

Purpose:

* List **legitimate ways to earn online with no upfront cost**

Sub‑categories:

```
/money/apps
/money/websites
/money/trading
```

Important framing:
❌ No “free money” claims
✅ “Earn online with time & effort — no investment”

---

## 5. Page Requirements

### 5.1 Game Page (Example: Clash Royale)

URL:
`/games/clash-royale`

Sections:

1. Current free rewards (updated)
2. Upcoming events with rewards
3. How to get free items legitimately
4. Tips & common mistakes
5. FAQ (SEO)

Rules:

* Only official Supercell sources
* No hacks, generators, or exploits
* Clear disclaimers

---

### 5.2 Money Page (Example: Websites)

URL:
`/money/websites`

Each listing must include:

* Website name
* What you do to earn
* Time required
* Country availability
* Payout method
* Minimum payout
* Pros / cons
* Trust rating (1–5)

---

## 6. Trust & Safety Requirements

### Mandatory

* Disclaimer on every page
* “No affiliation” notice where required
* No misleading promises
* Clear update timestamps

### SEO Trust Signals

* Author/about page
* Contact page
* Update dates
* Transparent criteria

---

## 7. Monetization

### Phase 1 (MVP)

* Google AdSense
* Native display ads

### Phase 2

* Affiliate links (trading platforms, apps)
* Email newsletter
* Telegram channel

---

## 8. MVP Scope (7 Days)

### Included

* Homepage
* Games hub
* Clash Royale page
* Money hub
* 1 money subpage
* Basic SEO

### Excluded (Later)

* User accounts
* Comments
* Notifications
* Paid memberships

---

## 9. SEO Strategy

* Keyword‑based URLs
* FAQ schema
* Freshness updates
* Year‑based titles (2026)
* Internal linking between hubs

Example title:

> Free Clash Royale Rewards (Updated 2026)

---

## 10. Tech Stack (Suggested)

* Frontend: Next.js
* Styling: Tailwind
* CMS: Markdown / Notion / Headless CMS
* Hosting: Vercel
* Analytics: Google Analytics + Search Console

---

## 11. Success Metrics

* Indexed pages
* Organic impressions
* Time on page
* Pages per session
* Ad RPM

---

## 12. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk             | Mitigation                       |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Google distrust  | Clean language, legit sources    |
| Ad rejection     | Avoid scam wording               |
| Legal issues     | Use disclaimers & official links |
| Low SEO traction | Focus on single niche first      |

---

## 13. Future Expansion

* More games
* Country‑specific pages
* Free trials & SaaS offers
* Browser extension

---

## 14. Summary

FreeThings is a **trust‑first content platform** that focuses on:

* Legit freebies
* Clear explanations
* Scalable SEO structure

If executed cleanly, it can grow into a **high‑traffic ad‑monetized authority site**.
